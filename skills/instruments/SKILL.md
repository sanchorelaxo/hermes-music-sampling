## Kastle 2 Note

The Kastle 2 ships with two swappable firmware modes — **FX Wizard** and **Wave Bard** — sold as separate instruments. They share the same hardware (patchbay, LFO, tempo gen, MIDI, USB) but are fundamentally different in function. Each has its own skill above.
